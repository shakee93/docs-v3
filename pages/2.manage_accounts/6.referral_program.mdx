# Referral Program

## Appreciate RapidLoad? Spread the Word and Reap the Benefits!

If RapidLoad is helping you, share it with others! Join our referral program and earn rewards for each successful referral. Help others experience faster websites and enjoy the benefits yourself!

  

## Tell a Friend and Earn Rewards!

Invite your friends to RapidLoad and earn up to $60 in cash back! Your friends get a 10% discount on their subscription, and you earn 10% of their plan’s value as a reward. It’s that easy!

## How to Get Started:

*   **Create Your Account:**

Follow the simple steps to set up your RapidLoad account.

*   **Invite Friends:**

Share your unique referral link with friends or invite them via email.

*   **Earn $60 Cash Back:**

For each friend who joins RapidLoad using your link, you’ll earn up to $60 in cashback (they get a 10% discount).

  

## How it Works

*   **Sign In:** Log in to your RapidLoad account.
*   **Refer & Earn:** Go to the "Refer & Earn" section in your dashboard.
*   **Share:** Copy your unique referral link or send email invitations directly from the platform.
*   **Track:** Monitor your referrals to see which are pending or completed.
*   **Earn:** Receive cashback or credits for successful referrals, which can be used to reduce your subscription cost.
