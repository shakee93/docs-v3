<details>
  <summary className="faq-item">What is RapidLoad?</summary>
  <p className="faq-answer">RapidLoad is a WordPress plugin designed to optimize your website's speed by removing unused CSS, minifying files, enabling caching, serving assets through a CDN, and more. It helps improve your Core Web Vitals and overall performance.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary className="faq-item">How do I download RapidLoad?</summary>
  <p className="faq-answer">You can download RapidLoad from your account dashboard after purchasing a license. If you’re a new user, visit our website, sign up, and follow the installation instructions provided. [learn more](/getting-started/installation)</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary className="faq-item">Where is my account?</summary>
  <p className="faq-answer">You can access your account by logging into [app.rapidload.io](https://app.rapidload.io/auth/sign-in). If you have trouble finding it, check your email for the sign-up confirmation or reset your password if needed.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary className="faq-item">How do I connect my site to RapidLoad?</summary>
  <p className="faq-answer">After installing the plugin, to activate it can either
  enter your license key or use the auto-activation feature. Once connected, you can configure optimization 
  settings directly from your WordPress dashboard.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary className="faq-item">How do I get a refund?</summary>
  <p className="faq-answer">RapidLoad offers a refund policy based on specific conditions. If you’re eligible for a refund, you can request one through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary className="faq-item">What is Test Mode?</summary>
  <p className="faq-answer">Test Mode allows you to preview how RapidLoad optimizations will affect your website before applying them live. This helps you fine-tune settings and ensure compatibility without impacting visitors.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary className="faq-item">What is AI Diagnostics?</summary>
  <p className="faq-answer">AI Diagnostics is a feature in RapidLoad that analyzes your website’s performance issues and provides automated recommendations to enhance speed and efficiency. It helps identify bottlenecks and suggests optimizations tailored to your site.</p>
</details>
