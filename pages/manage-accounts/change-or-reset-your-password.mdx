# Change or Reset Your Password

If you've forgotten your password, follow these simple steps to reset it:

<img style={{ marginTop: '24px' }} 
src="/images/reset-password.png" alt="Reset Password" />

1. Go to the [RapidLoad login](https://app.rapidload.ai/auth/sign-in) page.
2. Click on the **"Forgot Password"** link.
3. Enter the email address linked to your RapidLoad account and click **"Send Reset Email."**
4. Check your **email inbox** for the password reset email.
5. Open the email and click the **provided link** to create a new password.
6. After updating your password, log in to your RapidLoad account using your new credentials.

if you wish to change your current password, follow these steps:

<img style={{ marginTop: '24px' }} 
src="/images/update-password.png" alt="Update Password" />

1. Log in to your **RapidLoad** account.
2. Navigate to the **Profile** section in your dashboard.
3. Locate the **Update Password** option.
4. Enter your new password and confirm it by re-entering.
5. Click **Save Changes** to securely update your password.

  

Your password will now be updated successfully.
