# Login

RapidLoad offers three convenient ways to sign in:

<img style={{ marginTop: '24px' }} 
src="/images/login_form.png" alt="Login" />

## 1. Google

<img style={{ marginTop: '24px' }} 
src="/images/sign-in-google.png" alt="Login" />

Click the "**Sign in with Google**" button and select the email associated with your RapidLoad account to log in seamlessly.

## 2. Email and Password

<img style={{ marginTop: '24px' }} 
src="/images/sign-in-password.png" alt="Login" />

Enter the email registered with RapidLoad along with the password you created during sign-up to access your account.

## 3. Magic Link

<img style={{ marginTop: '24px' }} 
src="/images/magic-link.png" alt="Login" />

Enter your registered email and click "**Send Magic Link**." A one-time login link will be sent to your email. Click the link within 60 seconds to securely 
sign in to the RapidLoad app.
